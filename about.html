A little About me

How I got into Photography

Growing up I always loved to take pictures. I always just took random pictures of landscape as my family and I drove across the country. But it wasn't until highschool when I really began to get into photography for real

My Style of photography

My style of photography is very unique. I love when the pictures I take of people tell a story. As well as allow you to see within this person's life and personality and know them better. It is always very fun getting to know each person and helping to document who they are within a few pictures

My Vision as a Photographer

My goal in doing these photoshoots isn't just to help portray people as who they truly are but to help them to realize who they are and to understand their worth
